# RAG 101

In this section you'll get the conceptual foundations and hands-on experience with the core components:

* **Intro to RAG** — what retrieval-augmented generation is and why it matters
* **Understanding RAG** — how embeddings, similarity search, and context injection work together
* **Vector Stores & Milvus** — deploying and querying a production-grade vector database
* **Doc Intelligence with Docling** — processing complex PDFs and extracting structured content for ingestion
